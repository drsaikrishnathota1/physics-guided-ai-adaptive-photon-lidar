\section*{Limitations}

The reported study is simulation based and evaluates a limited number of
episodes. The optical channel is homogeneous within each episode, and every
principal baseline episode contains one target. Therefore, the reported
false-focus metric must not be interpreted as a complete target-absent
false-alarm probability.

The information-guided allocation rule is an interpretable physics-guided
heuristic rather than an exact mutual-information optimizer. No neural,
reinforcement-learning, quantum-computing, or experimentally demonstrated
quantum-enhancement claim is made.

The current results demonstrate numerical feasibility and comparative photon
efficiency. Experimental validation with measured single-photon LiDAR data
is reserved for future work.
