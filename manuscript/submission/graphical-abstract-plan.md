between short and long range.

- weak target return;
- SPAD receiver.

1. photon-count histogram;
2. posterior target probabilities;
3. predicted optical signal-to-background ratios;
4. physics-guided information score;
5. selection of the next region.

Use the conceptual score:

S_i = uncertainty × predicted optical contrast × exploration.

Do not label the method as artificial intelligence or exact expected
information gain.
